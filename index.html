Hello, I'm Mohammad Hossein

I am currently seeking a PhD position, working under the supervision of Professor Mostafa Milani at Western University. My research focuses on fairness in AI and entity matching. Specifically, I explore methods to ensure responsible AI through fair data management and post-processing techniques. I have worked on fairness calibration using optimal transport and developed new threshold-independent fairness metrics in entity matching systems. My research aims to mitigate biases in AI systems and ensure that outcomes are fair and equitable across different groups.
